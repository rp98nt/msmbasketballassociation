Our Achievements

Successfully organised and completed Maharashtra State teams coaching camps and arranged to participate teams in national levels tournaments of BFI in all respects. Successfully organised and completed Maharashtra State Inter District State Basketball tournaments, Successfully Invited eminent coaches and players for proper Basketball Training for upcoming talents. So many times arranged professional basketball activities for development of basketball talents. Provided hobby basketball coaches in various schools, colleges and academies also.

Aims and objectives

Talent search from urban, rural and Slam areas and give them proper training to develop their basketball skills to stand them on state, national and international level competition. Arrange basketball training camps regularly for basic fundamentals and advanced techniques and skills. Regularly arrange and organise various age groups of individual and group competition/tournaments on district State and national levels.
